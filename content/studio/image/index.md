## Features

- **Client-side processing** - your images never leave your browser
- **Real-time preview** with smooth animations
- **Dark mode support** throughout
- **Responsive design** for all devices
- **High-quality exports** with all edits applied
- **Built with Vue 3, Nuxt, and TypeScript**
